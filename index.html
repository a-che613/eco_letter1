The coding train is moving!

We have finished developing several features for our platform, including the authentication feature for both owners and agents, the EcoPoints feature, the Book Now feature, and the Blog and Daily Tips feature. These features are now ready to be deployed and used by our users

...and here comes the new features.....

This week, we plan to work on implementing these features next to improve the functionality and user experience of our platform: Notification feature, Owner Profile feature, Book Later and Recurrent Booking feature, Agent Tracking feature, Bidding feature, and Contact Us feature

No progress without challenges

For maintenance and scalability reasons, the idea of doing 2 separate apps for the Owners and Agents was considered. But due to other non-technical factors we are moving ahead with a single app both for users and agents.

Since we want a more scalable and robust version of the app, the front-end and back-end architecture had to be changed entirely - affecting the initial timeline for the MVP Launch
